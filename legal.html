TOKEN LEBNANI V2 LTD - PRE-APPLICATION LEGAL FRAMEWORK

DOCUMENT STATUS: Draft - For FSRA Pre-Application Meeting Only
CONFIDENTIALITY: Commercial in Confidence

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Token Lebnani V2 Ltd ("TLB") proposes a three-tier legal structure to facilitate regulated investment in Lebanese real estate through an ADGM-licensed Private Financing Platform. This memo outlines the legal framework for cross-border enforcement, asset protection, and regulatory compliance between ADGM and Lebanese jurisdictions.

Primary Legal Mechanisms:

  1. ADGM SPV ownership of Lebanese SARL for asset segregation
  2. Treaty-based enforcement via the 1958 New York Convention
  3. Registered security interests under Lebanese law

2. THREE-TIER STRUCTURE

TIER 1: TLB LTD (ADGM)

  • Legal Form: ADGM Private Company Limited by Shares
  • Regulatory Status: Applicant for Category 4 Financial Services Permission
  • FSRA Reference: COBS 18.5.1(b) - PFP Activity Limitation
  • Function: Arranges deals in investments; does not hold custody or provide advice

TIER 2: PROJECT SPV (ADGM)

  • Legal Form: ADGM Special Purpose Vehicle
  • Purpose: Structured for bankruptcy remoteness (subject to Non-Consolidation Legal Opinion at operational stage)
  • Ownership: Investor-owned via tokenized securities
  • Control Mechanism: Management Services Agreement with TLB Ltd
  • FSRA Reference: GEN 6 - Financial Soundness

TIER 3: LEBANESE SARL

  • Legal Form: Lebanese Société à Responsabilité Limitée
  • Purpose: Holds legal title to Lebanese real estate
  • Ownership: 100% owned by Project SPV
  • Registration: Lebanese Commercial Register (Ministry of Economy)
  • Compliance: Decree No. 11614/1969 (foreign ownership restrictions)

3. ENFORCEMENT MECHANISM

LEGAL BASIS: NEW YORK CONVENTION

  • Lebanon: Law No. 440 of 1998 (Official Gazette No. 50, 1998)
  • UAE: Federal Law No. 6 of 2018 (Official Gazette No. 630, 2018)
  • Both jurisdictions maintain reciprocity reservations

ARBITRATION FRAMEWORK:

  • Seat: ADGM Arbitration Centre
  • Rules: UNCITRAL Arbitration Rules 2013
  • Language: English
  • Governing Law: ADGM Law (where permissible), supplemented by Lebanese law for property matters

ENFORCEMENT PATHWAY:

  1. ADGM-seated arbitration award issuance
  2. Lebanese Court of Appeals recognition proceedings (exequatur)
  3. Potential challenges under:
    • Article V(2)(b) public policy exceptions
    • Procedural irregularities under Lebanese Code of Civil Procedure (Articles 793-814)
  4. Final execution via Lebanese bailiff (huissier de justice)

CONTINGENCY MEASURES:

  • Retention of Lebanese enforcement counsel
  • Parallel proceedings strategy for timely enforcement

4. LEBANON-SPECIFIC RISK MITIGATION

ASSET PROTECTION:

  1. Security Registration:
    • Registered share pledge at Lebanese Commercial Register
    • Dual-signatory requirements for SARL bank accounts
    • Property insurance with international carriers
  2. Local Counsel Retention:
    • Pre-vetted Lebanese law firms with real estate and commercial litigation expertise

CASH-FLOW REPATRIATION STRATEGY:

  • Primary Structure: Direct USD/AED payments to ADGM SPV accounts
  • Commercial Qualification: Subject to:
    • Banque du Liban Circular 150 compliance
    • Lebanese Income Tax Law (Article 56) withholding requirements
    • Applicable BDL circulars on currency conversion
  • Fallback Structure: Lebanese banking channel with outward remittance approvals

ASSET SELECTION CRITERIA:

  1. Properties compliant with Decree No. 11614/1969:
    • Maximum 3,000 square meters for foreign ownership
    • Clear title verification via Conservation Foncière
    • No existing encumbrances or third-party rights
  2. Due Diligence Requirements:
    • Independent RICS valuation (COBS 18.4 compliance)
    • Lebanese notary public title verification
    • Local zoning and planning compliance checks

5. REGULATORY ALIGNMENT

ADGM REQUIREMENTS ADDRESSED:

  1. GEN 2.3: Fit and Proper Test - Controlled functions matrix (see Document 2)
  2. COBS 18.4: Due Diligence - Triple-lock asset vetting process
  3. MKT 4.3: Marketing and disclosure - Risk disclaimers for Lebanese market
  4. FRT Framework 2026: Technology router model only (no custody or issuance)

LEBANESE COMPLIANCE ACKNOWLEDGED:

  1. Commercial Register: SARL formation requirements
  2. Tax Registration: Ministry of Finance requirements
  3. Real Estate Registration: Conservation Foncière procedures
